About Me

Hello, my name is Celi Macêdo. I am from Salvador, Bahia, Brazil. I like to hear good music, my favorite artist is Tears for Fears. I love seafood and baiana cuisine. "Vatapá" and "carurú" are on my list of the 10 best food. I enjoy scuba diving and outdoor activities, such as visiting isles, mountains, and forests. I am a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of the Latter-days Saints.
